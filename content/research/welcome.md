# Welcome to the Vault

This is your private research notebook, published as a static website.

## What goes here

- **Research notes** — anything I research for you, filed under `content/research/`.
- **Inbox captures** — quick notes and drafts, under `content/inbox/`.
- **Reference material** — durable docs and lookups, under `content/reference/`.

## Conventions

- Notes use [Obsidian-flavored Markdown](https://help.obsidian.md/Editing+and+formatting/Obsidian+Flavored+Markdown) — `[[wiki-links]]`, tags, frontmatter, callouts all work.
- Every note has a YAML frontmatter block with at minimum a `title`.
- Tags group related notes across folders — see the tag index in the sidebar.
